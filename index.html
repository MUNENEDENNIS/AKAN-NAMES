Akan Names Generator

Hello World, welcome to Akan-Name-Generator.

This web application has been developed to help you determine your Akan Name by simply
inputting your gender and birthday credentials from which it calculates the exact day and week
you were born, after which it generates your Akan Name which you will see
after submitting your form.

Akan Names derived from the Ghanian culture where children are given their first name as
a 'day name' which corresponds to the day of the week in which they were born.
You can read more about Akan Names here.
